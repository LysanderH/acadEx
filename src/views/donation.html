Are you a Cisco Networking Academy teaching CCNA ? Are you struggling to get equipment to teach CCNA at your academy ? Or are you having equipment that you do not use anymore at your academy ?

The 5 steps to make a donation

  1. Register

    Register on the platform. Verify that your information is correct under My profile

  2. Describe

    Describe the equipment that you will donate

  3. Test

    Test the equipment to be sure it is fully functional

  4. Connect

    You will be contacted by an academy requiring the type of equipment you wish to donate after the donation has been approved by a Cisco CSR representativ.

  5. Send

    Agree on the method to send equipment. We can help you with the step if you need to.
